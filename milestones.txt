The KidsHealth "Milestones" page provides information about developmental milestones for children from birth through adolescence. It outlines key physical, cognitive, emotional, and social milestones that children are expected to reach at various ages. The page offers guidance on what to look for at each stage of development and provides insights into typical growth patterns and behaviors. It also includes tips for parents on how to support their child's development and when to seek professional advice if there are concerns about developmental progress. This resource helps parents understand and monitor their child's development and ensures they are aware of important milestones and potential developmental issues.

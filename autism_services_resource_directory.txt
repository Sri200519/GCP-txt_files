The "Connecticut Collaborative Autism Services Resource Directory," published by the Connecticut Department of Public Health (DPH), is a comprehensive guide designed to assist families, caregivers, and professionals in finding autism-related resources across Connecticut. The directory is organized into sections that include Medical Services, Educational Services, Behavioral Services, Community and Support Services, Financial Assistance, and Recreational Services. Each section provides detailed contact information, descriptions of services, and geographical coverage, making it easier for users to locate specific types of support.

The Autism Speaks page on the signs of autism provides detailed information on the early indicators of autism spectrum disorder (ASD). It outlines common signs and symptoms across different age ranges, including developmental delays in communication, social interaction difficulties, and repetitive behaviors. The page offers guidance on what to look for in young children, such as challenges with eye contact, responding to their name, or engaging in pretend play. It emphasizes the importance of early identification and evaluation for timely intervention. Additionally, the page provides resources for parents and caregivers on how to seek a professional diagnosis and access support services.

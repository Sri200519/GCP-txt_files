The Birth23 website provides a comprehensive directory of programs and services for children with developmental needs and their families, emphasizing early intervention and support. The main page features a searchable list of programs categorized by town and program type. This allows users to find specific services tailored to their geographical location and the type of support needed, such as early intervention, special education, or therapy services. The directory includes a variety of programs, ranging from developmental screenings and educational support to therapy options like speech, occupational, and physical therapy. Each program listing typically includes a description of the services offered, eligibility criteria, and contact information for further inquiries.

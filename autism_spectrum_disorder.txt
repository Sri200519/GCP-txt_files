The "Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD)" page on the Connecticut Children's website offers detailed information about autism spectrum disorder and the services available for diagnosis and treatment at Connecticut Children's. Additionally, it offers information about the clinic's services, such as behavioral therapy, developmental assessments, and parent resources, emphasizing the commitment to providing comprehensive and compassionate care for children with autism.

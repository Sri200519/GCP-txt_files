The Connecticut Department of Developmental Services' "Family Support and Services" page provides detailed information about the support programs available for families of individuals with developmental disabilities. It outlines various services designed to assist families, including respite care, family training, and support groups. The page describes how these programs help alleviate the challenges faced by families, offering both financial assistance and practical support. Each service is explained with information on eligibility, application processes, and contact details, ensuring that families can access the necessary resources and support to enhance their quality of life and caregiving experience.

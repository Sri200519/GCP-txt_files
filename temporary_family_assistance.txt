The Connecticut Department of Social Services' "Temporary Family Assistance (TFA)" page provides detailed information about the TFA program, which offers financial support to low-income families. The page outlines eligibility criteria, including income and family size requirements, and describes the types of assistance available, such as cash benefits to help with basic needs. It explains the application process, including how to apply and the documentation required. Additionally, the page provides information on program rules, benefit amounts, and how to manage and renew assistance. Resources and contact details are also included to help families access and navigate the program effectively.

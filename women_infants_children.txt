The Connecticut Department of Public Health's "WIC" page provides information about the Women, Infants, and Children (WIC) program, which offers nutritional support to eligible low-income pregnant women, new mothers, and young children. The page details the services provided, including access to nutritious foods, breastfeeding support, and nutrition education. It outlines eligibility criteria, which include income limits and specific categorical requirements. The page also explains how to apply for WIC benefits, the types of food and support available, and the process for receiving and using benefits. Contact information is provided for further assistance and to help individuals navigate the application and enrollment process.

Autism.org provides a detailed explanation of autism as a neurodevelopmental condition impacting communication, behavior, and social interaction. The page outlines how autism is a spectrum disorder, meaning symptoms can range from mild to severe, including difficulties in understanding social cues, repetitive behaviors, intense focus on specific topics, and unusual responses to sensory input. The site discusses potential causes, including genetic factors and environmental triggers, and emphasizes the significance of early intervention and personalized therapies. Resources are offered for diagnosis, treatment, and support.

The Connecticut State Education Resource Center (SERC) "Services" page provides an overview of the various educational support services offered to schools and educators in Connecticut. It details a range of services designed to enhance educational practices, including professional development, technical assistance, and training on special education and inclusive practices. The page describes how SERC supports educators with resources and strategies to improve classroom instruction, address diverse learning needs, and implement effective educational programs. It also includes information on consulting services, workshops, and collaborative projects aimed at advancing educational outcomes and supporting student success.

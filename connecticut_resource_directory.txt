The Connecticut Office of the Child Advocate's "Resource List" page offers a comprehensive directory of support services available for children and families throughout Connecticut. It includes a range of resources such as mental health services for psychological support and counseling, educational support including special education and tutoring, legal aid for issues related to child protection and custody, and advocacy services to assist with children's rights and system navigation. Each entry in the list provides a brief description of the service along with contact information, making it easier for families to access the support they need and connect with relevant organizations.

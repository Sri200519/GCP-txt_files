The National Institute of Mental Health's (NIMH) page on Autism Spectrum Disorders (ASD) provides comprehensive information about the condition. It covers the definition and symptoms of ASD, including its impact on behavior, communication, and social interactions. The page discusses potential causes and risk factors associated with autism, as well as the importance of early diagnosis and intervention. It outlines various treatment and support options, such as behavioral therapies and educational interventions, and highlights ongoing research efforts to better understand and address ASD. Additionally, the page offers resources for finding support and staying informed about the latest scientific developments in the field.

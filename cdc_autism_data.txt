The CDC's "Autism Data & Research" page provides detailed information about autism research and data collected by the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC). The page offers access to various datasets and reports related to autism spectrum disorder (ASD), including prevalence statistics, research findings, and insights into the trends and factors associated with autism. It includes information on ongoing studies, data collection methods, and key research initiatives. Users can explore reports and data summaries that highlight the impact of ASD on different populations and regions, as well as access resources for understanding the latest scientific findings and public health strategies related to autism.
